I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Geography Department at the University of California Los-Angeles (UCLA). My research looks into the intricate relationship between past climates, human activity, wildfires, and vegetation dynamics. At UCLA, I am  investigating the historical impacts of climate change on California wildfires spanning thousands of years. I am also a member of the National Science Policy Network and her research interests involve identifying policies aimed at mitigating these environmental threats.
